There are a few things you should know before you play.


Skill Levels

Javelin has 5 different skill levels that players can play at, Beginner, Novice, Intermediate, Advanced, and Expert. We encourage our players to get familiar with what each skill level means.
View the difference between all the skill levels at: www.javelinsportsinc.com/skill-level


Cancellation Policy

If a game does not have 8 players signed up 2 hours before the game's start time, the game will be cancelled. Players who signed up will not be charged. You can cancel your attendance up to 4 hours before a game's start time without being charged. You can add your friends who are not on Javelin on the checkout page by adding a guest.
